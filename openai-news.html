About OpenAI

Founded in 2015 by Sam Altman, Elon Musk, Greg Brockman, John Schulman, Ilya Sutskever, and Wojciech Zaremba, OpenAI began as a non-profit AI research laboratory with the mission to ensure artificial general intelligence benefits all of humanity. The organization later created a capped-profit company, OpenAI LP, to attract additional funding while maintaining its core mission.

OpenAI has released several influential research papers and AI systems, with ChatGPT becoming one of the fastest-growing consumer applications in history after its release in November 2022.
