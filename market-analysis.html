# 📈 SaaS MARKET ANALYSIS

Market Overview



The global SaaS market reached $408.21 billion in 2025 and is projected to reach $465.03 billion in 2026, representing approximately 13.9% year-over-year growth[1][2]. The market is expected to expand at a compound annual growth rate (CAGR) of 12.85% through 2035, reaching approximately $1.37 trillion by 2035[2].

Key Growth Drivers:

•AI Integration: AI software revenue surged from $9.5 billion in 2018 to $118.6 billion in 2025[1]. The global AI SaaS market is expected to grow at a 38.28% CAGR from $71.54 billion in 2023 to $775.44 billion by 2031[3].
•Enterprise Adoption: 99% of organizations now use at least one SaaS application as of 2025[1]. Enterprise SaaS spending averages $52 million per year, up from $45 million in 2024[1].
•Software as Fastest-Growing IT Category: Gartner identifies software as the fastest-growing IT spending category for 2026, with 14.7% year-over-year growth[1].

Geographic Distribution:

North America represents the largest regional market[2]. The U.S. SaaS market alone was valued at $132.19 billion in 2025 and is projected to reach $451.05 billion by 2035 at a 13.06% CAGR[2]. Asia-Pacific accounts for approximately 20% of global SaaS revenue[4].

Industry Performance



Market Consolidation:

The average number of SaaS applications per company decreased from 112 in 2023 to 106 in 2024, though the consolidation rate has slowed significantly from 14% to just 5% year-over-year[3]. Mid-sized firms (1,500–4,999 employees) reduced their SaaS app portfolios by 29% in 2025[3].

Spending Trends:

SaaS spending grew 18% year-over-year from 2024 to 2025, substantially outpacing overall IT spending growth of 8%[1]. Spending on AI-native SaaS applications increased 108% year-over-year[4]. Global SaaS end-user spending grew 19.1% year-over-year[3].

Cloud Infrastructure:

Worldwide public cloud spending reached $723.4 billion in 2025, up from $595.7 billion in 2024[1]. IDC forecasts SaaS applications will capture over 40% of public cloud spending in 2024, with a 16.5% CAGR through 2028[3].

Emerging Opportunities



Vertical SaaS and Cybersecurity:

Vertical SaaS and cybersecurity represent the most promising growth sectors, commanding premium valuations in the 2026 market[7]. IDC forecasts Asia-Pacific security spending will reach $39.5 billion in 2026, with a projected 10% CAGR through 2029[4].
