📈 SaaS MARKET ANALYSIS - June 05, 2026

Market Overview
•The global SaaS market is estimated at $435.41 billion in 2026 and is projected to reach $976.61 billion by 2031 at a 17.55% CAGR.[2]
•Alternative market estimates are somewhat lower or higher depending on methodology: one forecast puts the market at $375.57 billion in 2026 and $1.48 trillion by 2034 at 18.7% CAGR, while another projects $465.03 billion in 2026 and 13.32% CAGR through 2034.[3][6]
•AI-native SaaS, usage-based pricing, and security/compliance software are among the clearest demand drivers in 2026, with security/compliance applications growing at 24.1% CAGR and usage-based pricing at 27.9% CAGR in one market breakdown.[2]
•Enterprise buying is still concentrated in large organizations, which represented 58.05% of SaaS market demand in 2025, but SMEs are the fastest-growing buyer cohort at 19.2% CAGR.[2]
•Geography remains concentrated in North America, which held 42.60% of global SaaS share in 2025 in one report and 46.9% in another; Asia-Pacific is the fastest-growing region at 18.7% CAGR.[2][3]
•Vertical SaaS is a major subsegment, estimated at $143.45 billion in 2026 and projected to reach $499.42 billion by 2035, with North America at 48–52% share and Europe at 30–33%.[1]
