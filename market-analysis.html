📈 SaaS MARKET ANALYSIS - June 23, 2026

Market Overview
The global Software As A Service (SaaS) market is estimated at USD 435.41 billion in 2026, growing from USD 370.4 billion in 2025, with projections to reach USD 976.61 billion by 2031 at a 17.55% CAGR[1]. This expansion is driven by enterprise cloud migration, rapid AI integration, and the attractive economics of pay-as-you-go delivery models[1]. North America remains the largest market, commanding 42.60% of the share in 2025, while Asia Pacific is the fastest-growing region with a projected 18.7% CAGR through 2031[1]. The IT and Telecom vertical leads with a 22.45% share, whereas Healthcare is the fastest-growing vertical at 22.9% CAGR due to digital health and compliance needs[1]. Public cloud deployment dominates with 89.42% of the market share in 2025[1].

Industry Performance
Key market leaders include Salesforce, Oracle Corp., SAP SE, Rackspace Technology Inc., and VMware Inc.[4]. The productivity software segment is expected to grow at a significant 10.6% CAGR[4].
