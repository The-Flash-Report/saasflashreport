### Essential SaaS Tools for Startups

#### CRM & Sales
1. HubSpot CRM
- Pricing: Free (basic), $45/month (Starter)
- Key Features: Contact management, sales pipeline tracking, email templates
- Target Company Size: Small to medium-sized businesses

2. Salesforce Essentials
- Pricing: $25/user/month (billed annually)
- Key Features: Lead management, sales forecasting, mobile app
- Target Company Size: Small to medium-sized businesses

3. Zoho CRM
- Pricing: Free (3 users), $12/user/month (Standard)
- Key Features: Lead scoring, sales automation, integration with other Zoho apps
- Target Company Size: Small to medium-sized businesses

#### Marketing Automation
1. MailChimp
- Pricing: Free (up to 2,000 subscribers), $11/month (Essentials)
- Key Features: Email marketing, automation workflows, landing pages
- Target Company Size: Small to medium-sized businesses

2. Hootsuite
- Pricing: $19/month (Professional), $49/month (Team)
- Key Features: Social media scheduling, content curation, analytics
- Target Company Size: Small to medium-sized businesses

3. Marketo
- Pricing: Custom pricing for businesses
- Key Features: Lead scoring, email marketing, customer journey mapping
- Target Company Size: Medium to large-sized businesses

#### Customer Support
1. Zendesk
- Pricing: $19/agent/month (Team), $49/agent/month (Professional)
- Key Features: Ticketing system, chat support, knowledge base
- Target Company Size: Small to large-sized businesses

2. Freshdesk
- Pricing: Free (Sprout), $15/agent/month (Blossom)
- Key Features: Ticket management, chat support, automation
- Target Company Size: Small to medium-sized businesses

3. Intercom
- Pricing: Custom pricing for businesses
- Key Features: Chatbots, customer messaging, user segmentation
- Target Company Size: Medium to large-sized businesses

### Enterprise SaaS Solutions

#### Business Intelligence
1. Tableau
- Pricing: $35/user/month (Creator), $12/user/month (Explorer)
- Key Features: Data visualization, reporting, integration with various data sources
- Target Company Size: Medium to large-sized businesses

2. Google Analytics 360
- Pricing: Custom pricing for enterprises
- Key Features: Advanced analytics, data integration, attribution modeling
- Target Company Size: Large-sized businesses

3. Mixpanel
- Pricing: Free (up to 1,000 users), $25/month (Growth)
- Key Features: User behavior tracking, A/B testing, funnels analysis
- Target Company Size: Small to medium-sized businesses

#### HR & Payroll
1. Workday
- Pricing: Custom pricing for enterprises
- Key Features: HR management, payroll processing, financial management
- Target Company Size: Large-sized businesses

2. PeopleForce
- Pricing: Custom pricing for businesses
- Key Features: HR management, performance tracking, recruitment tools
- Target Company Size: Medium to large-sized businesses

3. GoCo
- Pricing: Custom pricing for businesses
