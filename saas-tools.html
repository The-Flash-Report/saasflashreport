# Essential SaaS Tools for Startups



## CRM & Sales


1. HubSpot CRM
- *Pricing:* Free (core features), Pro plans from $13/user/month
- *Key Features:* Lead management, sales pipeline tracking, automation, robust integrations (Slack, Zapier, Stripe), AI-powered reporting
- *Target Size:* Startups, SMBs
- *Recent Update:* Enhanced AI-driven lead scoring and predictive analytics in 2025

2. Salesforce Essentials
- *Pricing:* $2.76/user/month (Essentials plan)
- *Key Features:* Contact management, workflow automation, third-party integrations, mobile access
- *Target Size:* Startups, small teams
- *Recent Update:* Improved mobile CRM experience and AI-powered insights

3. Freshsales (Freshworks)
- *Pricing:* Free plan available, paid plans from $15/user/month
- *Key Features:* AI-powered sales automation, pipeline management, built-in phone/email, real-time analytics
- *Target Size:* Early-stage startups, sales-driven teams
- *Recent Update:* New AI content generator for sales outreach

## Customer Support


1. Freshdesk (Freshworks)
- *Pricing:* Free plan, paid plans from $15/agent/month
- *Key Features:* Ticketing, knowledge base, chatbot, multi-channel support
- *Target Size:* Startups, SMBs
- *Recent Update:* AI-powered ticket routing and sentiment analysis

2. Intercom
- *Pricing:* Free plan, paid plans from $74/month
- *Key Features:* Live chat, help center, targeted messaging, automation
- *Target Size:* Startups, growth-stage companies
- *Recent Update:* AI-driven customer segmentation and proactive chat

3. Zendesk
- *Pricing:* Suite Team from $49/agent/month
- *Key Features:* Ticketing, knowledge base, analytics, integrations
- *Target Size:* SMBs, mid-market
- *Recent Update:* Enhanced AI-powered agent assist and reporting
