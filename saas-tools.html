# Essential SaaS Tools for Startups



## CRM & Sales


1. HubSpot CRM
- Pricing: Free (core CRM), Starter from $20/month (1K contacts or 1 user), Professional/Enterprise higher
- Key Features: User-friendly interface, free CRM with robust features, integrated marketing/sales automation, HubSpot Academy for training
- Target Size: Startups, SMBs

2. Pipedrive
- Pricing: Starts at $14.90/user/month (Essential), up to $99/user/month (Enterprise)
- Key Features: Visual sales pipeline, customizable workflows, real-time reporting, automation for sales actions
- Target Size: SMBs, sales-focused startups

3. Kendo AI
- Pricing: Custom pricing (early-stage startup, contact for demo)
- Key Features: AI-powered sales scenario simulation, multilingual support, real-time analytics, roleplay assessments for onboarding
- Target Size: SaaS/B2B startups scaling sales teams

---

## Marketing Automation


1. HubSpot Marketing Hub
- Pricing: Starter from $20/month, Professional from $800/month
- Key Features: Email marketing, lead nurturing, landing pages, analytics, seamless CRM integration
- Target Size: Startups, SMBs

2. Surfer SEO
- Pricing: Basic $89/month, Pro $159/month, Business $299/month
- Key Features: AI-powered content optimization, SERP analyzer, content editor, SEO audit, Surfer Academy for training
- Target Size: Content-driven startups, SMBs

3. Hunter
- Pricing: Free (limited searches), Starter $49/month, Business $99/month
- Key Features: Email finder, domain search, email verifier, cold outreach automation, extensive template library
- Target Size: Sales/marketing startups, SMBs

---

## Customer Support


1. Loom
- Pricing: Free (limited), Starter $10/user/month, Business $16/user/month
- Key Features: Async video messaging, screen recording, in-app feedback, integrations with Slack, Notion, etc.
- Target Size: Startups, remote teams

2. Zendesk
- Pricing: Suite Team $45/agent/month, Suite Growth $75/agent/month, Suite Professional $99/agent/month
- Key Features: Ticketing, live chat, knowledge base, AI-powered support, analytics
- Target Size: SMBs, scaling startups

3. Freshdesk
- Pricing: Free (up to 10 agents), Growth $15/agent/month, Pro $49/agent/month
- Key Features: Omnichannel support, automation, self-service portal, reporting
- Target Size: Startups, SMBs
