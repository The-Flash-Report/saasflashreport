# 🛠️ SaaS TOOL RECOMMENDATIONS

Essential SaaS Tools for Startups



CRM & Sales

•HubSpot CRM — Starting from $15/month with a free tier, HubSpot offers contact/deals management, pipelines, CRM import/export, reports, and email automation.[4] Best for startups needing a feature-rich, scalable CRM solution.

•Snov.io — From $39/month with a free plan, this all-in-one platform includes cold email sequences, LinkedIn automation, email finder and verifier, sales CRM, email warm-up, and sales-trained AI.[4] Ideal for early-stage B2B teams focused on outreach automation.

•Intercom — Tiered pricing at approximately $29/seat/month (Essential), $85/seat/month (Advanced), and $132/seat/month (Expert), billed annually.[1] Offers customer communication and support features, though integration setup can be complex.

Marketing Automation

•Brevo — Starting from $9/month with a free plan, Brevo provides email/SMS/WhatsApp automation, campaign templates, transactional SMTP, and CRM lite functionality.[4] Best for teams launching multichannel marketing campaigns with simple automation.

•Meta Ads — Flexible pricing from $1 to $500 per day (billed for ads spent).[4] Ideal for B2C, e-commerce, or local service startups driving sales through paid social media.

•Mixpanel — From $0.28 per 1,000 events with a free plan, Mixpanel offers funnels, retention and conversion reporting, and alerts on metric changes.[4] Perfect for product teams measuring activations, retention, and other key events.

Customer Support

•Zendesk — Starting from $25/month with a free plan, Zendesk provides omnichannel tickets, CRM integration, self-service knowledge base, AI agents, and reports.[4] Best for startups scaling multichannel support operations.

•Stripe Billing — Uses a pay-as-you-go model at 0.7% of billing volume for recurring and usage-based billing.[1] Designed for startups managing subscription revenue, metered billing, trials, coupons, and invoices without building systems from scratch. Features include custom pricing plans (flat-rate, per-seat, usage-based, tiered, variable, multi-currency) and automated invoicing with tax calculations.[1]

Enterprise SaaS Solutions



Project Management

•Trello — From $5 per user with a free plan, Trello offers Kanban boards, Butler automation, and workflow templates.[4] Suitable for visual project management across small to mid-sized teams.

•ClickUp — Mentioned as a leading tool for 2026 startup stacks, though specific pricing details are not provided in available sources.[6]

Finance & Accounting

•Xero — Starting from $25/month (recently reduced to $2.50) with a free plan, Xero includes invoicing, live bank feeds, reconciliation, and multi-currency support.[4] Ideal for startups needing reliable finance management with automated reconciliation.
