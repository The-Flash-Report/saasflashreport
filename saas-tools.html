🛠️ SaaS TOOL RECOMMENDATIONS - March 24, 2026

Essential SaaS Tools for Startups
•CRM & Sales:
- Intercom (target: small teams, 1-50 users): Key features include tiered AI messaging, seat-based collaboration, and usage charges for AI resolutions like Fin AI. Pricing: Essential $29/seat/month (annual), Advanced $85/seat/month, Expert $132/seat/month + ~$0.99 per resolved conversation.[4]
- Stripe Billing (target: early-stage bootstrapped startups): Supports flat-rate, per-seat, usage-based, tiered, and multi-currency pricing with automated invoicing. Pricing: Pay-as-you-go at 0.7% of billing volume.[4]
- Notion (target: agile startups, 5-50 users): All-in-one workspace for sales pipelines, docs, and databases with recent AI feature tiers. Pricing: Free plan; Plus ~$9.50/user/month; Business ~$19.50/user/month (annual).[4]
•Marketing Automation:
- DeskTrack (target: lean marketing teams, <20 users): Time tracking integrated with productivity analytics for campaign optimization, pay-as-you-grow tiers. Pricing: Transparent startup plans with no upfront commitments.[5]
- Stripe Billing (target: growth-stage startups): Custom pricing models for automated campaigns, invoicing, and revenue tracking. Pricing: 0.7% of billing volume, scales with revenue.[4]
- Notion (target: solopreneurs to small teams): AI-powered content planning and automation workflows. Pricing: Free; Plus ~$9.50/user/month (annual).[4]
•Customer Support:
- Intercom (target: startups with customer-facing teams, 10-100 users): AI resolutions, messaging channels, and seat-based support tiers updated for 2026 AI workloads. Pricing: Essential $29/seat/month; Advanced $85/seat/month + usage fees.[4]
- DeskTrack (target: remote support teams): Productivity monitoring for support efficiency. Pricing: Flexible pay-as-you-grow model.[5]
- Slack (target: small support squads, <50 users): Real-time chat with recent AI integrations for query routing. Pricing: Free basic; Pro tiers from ~$8.75/user/month (annual, based on standard models).[5]

Enterprise SaaS Solutions
•Business Intelligence:
- Stripe Billing (target: mid-to-large enterprises, 100+ users): Usage-based analytics for revenue intelligence and custom reporting. Pricing: 0.7% of volume + add-ons like Stripe Tax.[4]
- Notion (target: scaling enterprises): Enterprise custom with advanced AI BI features and integrations. Pricing: Custom beyond Business ~$19.50/user/month.[4]
- DeskTrack (target: enterprise productivity tracking): Granular usage data for BI dashboards. Pricing: Scaled enterprise tiers.[5]
•HR & Payroll:
- Stripe Billing (target: enterprises with variable payroll): Hybrid usage/subscription for payroll processing and compliance. Pricing: 0.7% volume-based.[4]
- DeskTrack (target: distributed HR teams): Time tracking for payroll accuracy and compliance. Pricing: Enterprise pay-as-you-grow.[5]
- Notion (target: HR teams in large orgs): Custom HR workflows with recent AI updates. Pricing: Enterprise custom.[4]
•Project Management:
- Notion (target: enterprises, 50+ users): Feature-based tiers for advanced project tracking, whiteboards, and AI planning. Pricing: Business ~$19.50/user/month; Enterprise custom.[4]
- DeskTrack (target: project teams): Integrated time tracking for management. Pricing: Flexible scaling tiers.
