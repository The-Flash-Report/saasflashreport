🛠️ SaaS TOOL RECOMMENDATIONS - March 13, 2026

Essential SaaS Tools for Startups
•CRM & Sales:
- Pipedrive: Starts at $24/month; key features include sales pipeline management, automation, and email integration. Targets small teams and startups.[2]
- Stripe Billing: Pay-as-you-go at 0.7% of billing volume; supports flexible pricing models, automated invoicing, and dunning. Ideal for early-stage revenue ops.[3]
- Intercom: Essential plan at $29/seat/month (annual); offers AI chat, messaging, and usage-based add-ons like $0.99 per AI resolution. Suited for growth-stage startups.[3]
•Marketing Automation:
- Moosend: Pricing from entry plans (details on site); features email campaigns, automation workflows, and landing pages. Best for small marketing teams.[2]
- Semrush: Pro at $120/month; SEO tools, project tracking, and competitor analysis. Targets startups scaling content marketing.[2]
- Ahrefs: Starts at $99/month up to $999; site audits, keyword research, and backlink tracking. For SEO-focused startups.[2]
•Customer Support:
- Help Scout: Starts at $25/user/month; shared inbox, knowledge base, and reports. Fits small support teams.[2]
- Intercom: $29/seat/month base; AI resolutions and multi-channel support. Startup-friendly with scalable usage fees.[3]
- Support tool (unnamed in results): Starts at $19/user/month annual; advanced plans at $49/user. Targets growing customer service needs.[2]

- Amplitude: Freemium with seat-based paid; user behavior tracking and product growth insights. Enterprise product teams.[4]
