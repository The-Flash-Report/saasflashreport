### Essential SaaS Tools for Startups

CRM & Sales
1. HubSpot CRM
- Pricing: Free tier available; paid plans start at $50/month
- Key Features: Contact management, sales pipeline tracking, email automation, reporting dashboards
- Target: Startups to SMBs
2. HappyLoop
- Pricing: Custom pricing based on usage
- Key Features: Customer satisfaction tracking, feedback loops, engagement analytics
- Target: Early-stage startups focused on customer experience
3. QuickBooks
- Pricing: Plans from $25/month
- Key Features: Sales invoicing, payment processing, financial reporting
- Target: Startups needing integrated sales and accounting

Marketing Automation
1. MailChimp
- Pricing: Free tier; paid plans from $13/month
- Key Features: Email campaigns, audience segmentation, marketing analytics
- Target: Startups and SMBs
2. Hootsuite
- Pricing: Plans start at $49/month
- Key Features: Social media scheduling, monitoring, analytics
- Target: Startups managing social media presence
3. Notion Startup Plan
- Pricing: Startup plan pricing varies; free for eligible startups
- Key Features: All-in-one workspace with AI summarization, advanced permissions
- Target: Startups needing marketing content collaboration and planning

Customer Support
1. Zendesk
- Pricing: Plans start at $19/agent/month
- Key Features: Ticketing system, multi-channel support, automation, reporting
- Target: Startups to enterprises
2. Intercom
- Pricing: Starting at $74/month
- Key Features: Live chat, customer messaging, bots, product tours
- Target: Startups focused on real-time customer engagement
3. Olark
- Pricing: $29/user/month
- Key Features: Live chat, automation rules, reporting
- Target: Small startups and SMBs
