🛠️ SaaS TOOL RECOMMENDATIONS - June 27, 2026

Essential SaaS Tools for Startups

•CRM & Sales:
1. HubSpot CRM – Free tier; Paid tiers start at $20/user/month (Starter) to $150+/user/month (Enterprise). Key features: Unified customer database, email automation, sales pipeline tracking, and AI-driven insights. Best for: Pre-MVP to Scaling startups seeking an all-in-one solution [2].
2. LinkedIn Sales Navigator – ~$1,490/year per seat. Key features: Advanced lead filtering, real-time alerts, and integrated CRM data for high-value B2B prospecting. Best for: Scaling startups with dedicated sales teams [2].
3. Notion CRM (via Notion templates) – $10/user/month (Plus); $20/user/month (Business). Key features: Flexible database, seamless integration with Asana/GitHub/Slack, and modular workflow assembly. Best for: Pre-MVP to Post-launch teams prioritizing customization [2][3].

•Marketing Automation:
1. HubSpot Marketing Hub – Starts at $20/month (Starter); $1,600/month (Professional); $3,600/month (Enterprise). Key features: AI content generation, multi-channel email campaigns, SEO tools, and lead scoring. Best for: Startups needing robust automation [2].
2. Zapier – $19.99/month (Starter); $69/month (Professional). Key features: Automated workflows connecting 5,000+ apps, AI-powered logic, and no-code integration. Best for: Early-stage teams automating repetitive tasks [2].
3. Typeform – $29/month (Basic); $89/month (Plus). Key features: Conversational data collection, real-time analytics, and seamless CRM integrations. Best for: Startups focusing on customer feedback and lead capture [2].

•Customer Support:
1. Intercom – ~$29/month (Starter); ~$17,398/year avg spend for enterprise. Key features: AI chatbots, personalized messaging, ticketing system, and customer data platform. Best for: Scaling startups with high-volume support needs [2].
2. Zendesk – ~$19/month (Suite Team); ~$14,195/year avg spend for enterprise. Key features: Omnichannel support, AI automation, and robust reporting. Best for: Startups needing reliable ticket management [2].
3. PostHog – Free tier; $199/month (Pro). Key features: Event analytics, product telemetry, and support ticketing via data pipeline. Best for: Tech-focused startups integrating support with product insights [3].

Enterprise SaaS Solutions

•Business Intelligence:
