Essential SaaS Tools for Startups

•CRM & Sales
- HubSpot CRM — Free core CRM; premium features start at $20/month. Best for early-stage to mid-market teams needing an all-in-one CRM, lead management, and marketing automation; recent 2026 coverage still positions it as a top startup CRM.[2][1]
- Salesforce Starter / small-business editions — Pricing is not specified in the provided results; typically used by growing startups to enterprise teams needing a highly extensible CRM and sales workflow platform. It is listed among top business SaaS tools in 2026 coverage.[5]
- Stripe — Pricing is usage-based rather than a flat subscription in the provided results; best for startups to scale-ups that need payments, subscriptions, and billing infrastructure alongside sales operations.[5][1]

•Marketing Automation
- HubSpot Marketing Hub — Free CRM entry point with paid features starting at $20/month for premium capabilities. Best for startup and SMB marketing teams that want email, forms, automation, and lead nurturing in one stack.[2][5]

•Customer Support

- Zendesk — Pricing is not specified in the provided results; best for SMBs to enterprise support teams that need ticketing, chat, and structured customer service workflows.[5]
- BoldDesk — $99/month for 5 agents. Best for small to mid-sized support teams that want ticketing, AI Agent, AI Copilot, omnichannel inbox, knowledge base, and analytics.[4]
