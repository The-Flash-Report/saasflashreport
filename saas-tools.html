### Essential SaaS Tools for Startups

CRM & Sales
1. HappyLoop
- Pricing: Starts at $29/month
- Key Features: Customer satisfaction analytics, automated feedback loops, growth-focused insights
- Target: Early-stage startups aiming to boost customer retention and satisfaction[1]
2. Stripe Atlas
- Pricing: One-time fee $500 for company formation; additional fees for services
- Key Features: Fast global company formation, automated EIN issuance under 24 hours, Delaware franchise tax estimator
- Target: Startups needing quick, compliant incorporation and payment infrastructure[2]
3. HubSpot CRM (widely used, updated regularly)
- Pricing: Free tier available; paid plans from $50/month
- Key Features: Contact management, sales pipeline automation, email tracking, integration with marketing tools
- Target: Startups and SMBs looking for scalable CRM solutions

Marketing Automation
1. MailChimp
- Pricing: Free tier; paid plans start at $13/month
- Key Features: Email marketing, automation workflows, audience segmentation, analytics
- Target: Startups and small businesses focusing on email campaigns[1]
2. Hootsuite
- Pricing: Plans start at $49/month
- Key Features: Social media scheduling, monitoring, analytics, team collaboration
- Target: Startups managing multiple social channels efficiently[1]
3. Galaxy Launch Suite
- Pricing: Custom pricing based on features
- Key Features: Integrated marketing automation, launch management, analytics dashboard
- Target: Founders needing end-to-end launch and growth tools[2]

Customer Support
1. Zendesk
- Pricing: Starts at $19/agent/month
- Key Features: Multi-channel support, ticketing system, AI-powered chatbots, analytics
- Target: Startups and SMBs scaling customer service[3]
2. Intercom
- Pricing: Starts at $74/month
- Key Features: Live chat, customer engagement, product tours, automation
- Target: Startups focusing on personalized customer communication[3]
3. Olark
- Pricing: $29/user/month
- Key Features: Live chat, real-time reporting, CRM integrations
- Target: Small startups needing simple, effective chat support[3]

- Key Features: Web and app analytics, event tracking, AI insights
