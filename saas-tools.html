🛠️ SaaS TOOL RECOMMENDATIONS - March 02, 2026

Essential SaaS Tools for Startups
•CRM & Sales:
- HubSpot CRM (Startups, <50 employees): Free core CRM with unlimited users and up to 1M contacts; paid hubs start at $20–$50/month per product for automation, reporting, AI features.[1]
- Derrick (Startups, <20 employees): Google Sheets prospecting with 50+ enrichment attributes, AI lead scoring; Free (200 credits/month), Small at €9/month (4K credits).[2]
- Stripe Billing (Startups, revenue-focused): Flexible pricing models, dunning for payments; pay-per-revenue fees only, add-ons like Stripe Tax extra.[1]
•Marketing Automation:
- La Growth Machine (LGM) (Startups, <50 employees): Prospecting automation with Lookalike Search, LinkedIn Intent; Basic €50/month per identity, Pro €100, Ultimate €150 (unlimited leads).[2]
- Outreach (Growing startups, 20-100 employees): AI-driven workflows boosting productivity 60%, forecasting; Custom pricing, mid-tier €50-€150/month per user.[2]
- SaaSHero (Startups seeking execution): Demand gen with $504K ARR results, flat-fee; Starts at $1,250/month for up to $10K spend.[6]
•Customer Support:
- Intercom (Startups, <50 employees): Tiered messaging with AI; Essential $29/seat/month (annual), Advanced $85, Expert $132.[1]
- Jotform Tables/AI Agents (Solo to small teams): Form-based support, basic tables; Free Starter, paid from $34/month (submissions/storage).[1]
- Notion (Collaborative startups, <20 employees): All-in-one docs/support workflows; Free plan, Plus $9.50/user/month, Business $19.50 (annual).[1]

Enterprise SaaS Solutions
•Business Intelligence:
- Salesloft (Mid-large enterprises, 100+ employees): Sales engagement, forecasting, deal management; Custom pricing.[2]
- Gong/Outreach (Enterprises, 200+ employees): Revenue intelligence, AI workflows; Custom, thousands/month for teams.[2]
- Zylo (Enterprises managing sprawl): SaaS spend analytics, renewal tracking; Custom, financial-focused.[7]
•HR & Payroll:
- Tropic (Mid-large, 100+ employees): SaaS procurement with negotiation, 17% savings; Custom, integrates finance for global ops.[4]
- Spendflo (Enterprises with large budgets): Cost reduction 20-30%, contract tracking; Starts $2,500/month, expert-led.[5]
- Trelica (Mid-market enterprises, 50-500 employees): Usage dashboards, renewals; Mid-market pricing, easy setup.[7]
•Project Management:
- Zuora (Scaling enterprises, 200+): Billing/subscriptions with metering; Starts ~$75K/year, 3-6 month setup.[3]
- Chargebee (Enterprises, B2B/B2C): Multi-entity, Salesforce sync; Starter free to $250K billing then 0.75%, Performance $7,188/year.[3]
- Maxio (Growth enterprises): Usage-based billing; Custom plans from Standard $29/month (limited users).[3]
