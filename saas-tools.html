### Essential SaaS Tools for Startups

CRM & Sales
• HubSpot Starter CRM Suite
Pricing: Starts at $50/month
Key Features: Contact management, email tracking, deal pipelines, marketing automation, and integration with popular tools. Ideal for startups scaling sales operations.
Target: Small to medium startups[3].

• HappyLoop
Pricing: Custom pricing based on usage
Key Features: Customer engagement automation, feedback loops, and growth analytics to boost startup revenue and productivity.
Target: Early-stage startups aiming for growth[1].

• Stripe Atlas
Pricing: One-time fee of $500 for company formation + additional fees for services
Key Features: Fast company incorporation, automated EIN issuance (under 24 hours), tax estimators, and payment processing integration.
Target: Startups needing quick, compliant company formation and payment infrastructure[3].

Marketing Automation
• MailChimp
Pricing: Free tier available; paid plans start at $13/month
Key Features: Email marketing, audience segmentation, automation workflows, and analytics.
Target: Startups and SMBs looking for easy-to-use marketing automation[1].

• Hootsuite
Pricing: Plans start at $49/month
Key Features: Social media scheduling, monitoring, analytics, and team collaboration.
Target: Startups managing multiple social channels[1].

• Notion Startup Plan (2025)
Pricing: $8/user/month
Key Features: All-in-one workspace with advanced permissions, native AI summarization, and collaboration tools.
Target: Startups needing integrated marketing content planning and team collaboration[3].

Customer Support
• Zendesk
Pricing: Starts at $49/agent/month
Key Features: Multi-channel support, ticketing system, AI-powered chatbots, and analytics.
Target: Startups and SMBs scaling customer support[2].

• Intercom
Pricing: Starts at $74/month
Key Features: Live chat, customer messaging, automation, and product tours.
Target: Startups focused on personalized customer engagement[2].

• Olark
Pricing: $29/user/month
Key Features: Live chat, real-time reporting, and CRM integrations.
Target: Small startups needing simple, effective live chat support[2].

### Enterprise SaaS Solutions

Business Intelligence
• Tableau
Pricing: Starts at $70/user/month
Key Features: Data visualization, dashboard creation, real-time analytics, and integration with multiple data sources.
Target: Medium to large enterprises requiring advanced BI[2].

• Mixpanel
Pricing: Free tier available; paid plans start at $25/month
Key Features: User behavior analytics, funnel analysis, retention tracking, and A/B testing.
Target: Enterprises focused on product analytics and user engagement[2].

• Google Analytics 4 (GA4)
Pricing: Free; GA360 enterprise version available at custom pricing
Key Features: Web and app analytics, event tracking, AI insights, and integration with Google Ads.
Target: Enterprises needing comprehensive digital analytics[2].

HR & Payroll
• Workday
Pricing: Custom pricing (enterprise-focused)
Key Features: Human capital management, payroll, talent acquisition, and workforce planning.
Target: Large enterprises with complex HR needs[2].

• PeopleForce
Pricing: Starts at $5/user/month
Key Features: Recruitment, employee engagement, performance management, and payroll integration.
Target: Medium to large enterprises looking for scalable HR solution
