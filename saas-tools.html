Essential SaaS Tools for Startups
*(Target: Early-stage teams, pre-MVP to scaling; focus on modular, integrable tools from 2026 reviews)*

•CRM & Sales
1. La Growth Machine - Multichannel prospecting (LinkedIn/email/voice). *Pricing:* $40/mo. *Key features:* AI automation for replies, 14-day trial. *(Small teams, 1-50 users)* [2]
2. Apollo - All-in-one sales platform. *Pricing:* Free plan available. *Key features:* 210M+ contacts, built-in outreach. *(Startups, 1-100 users)* [2]
3. HubSpot Breeze - AI assistant for startups. *Pricing:* Free with CRM. *Key features:* Conversation summaries, meeting notes, automation. *(Pre-seed to Series A)* [1]

•Marketing Automation
1. Clay - Data enrichment & AI research. *Pricing:* $134/mo. *Key features:* 150+ integrations, waterfall enrichment. *(Growth startups, 10-50 users)* [2]
2. Snov.io - Affordable outreach. *Pricing:* $39/mo. *Key features:* 98% email verification, no-card trial. *(Bootstrapped teams)* [2]
3. Zapier - No-code workflows. *Pricing:* Free (100 tasks/mo). *Key features:* 8,000+ app integrations. *(Any small team)* [2]

•Customer Support
1. Jotform Tables - Modular data workspace. *Pricing:* Free Starter; $34/mo paid. *Key features:* Form-to-CRM, 200+ integrations, AI agents ($34/mo extra). *(Pre-MVP teams)* [1]
2. Help Scout (implied in stacks) - AI agents & shared inbox. *Pricing:* Starts ~$20/user/mo. *Key features:* Knowledge base, built-in CRM. *(Small support teams)* [4]
3. Tidio - Free live chat/ticketing. *Pricing:* Free core. *Key features:* Visitor monitoring, AI chatbots. *(Solo to 20 users)* [4]
