🛠️ SaaS TOOL RECOMMENDATIONS - April 19, 2026

Essential SaaS Tools for Startups
•CRM & Sales:
- HubSpot CRM (Free core CRM; $50/mo Marketing/Sales Hub starter): Free inbound CRM with sales pipelines, email tracking, and meeting scheduling. Targets startups (1-50 employees).[3]
- Zoho CRM (Free for 3 users; $14/user/mo standard): Multi-channel sales automation, AI lead scoring, and workflow builder. Ideal for small SaaS teams scaling sales.[1]
- Salesforce Essentials (Contact for pricing, ~$25/user/mo starter): Customizable pipelines and AI predictions. Suited for growing startups (10-100 employees).[1]
•Marketing Automation:
- Mailchimp (Free up to 500 contacts; $13/mo essentials): Email campaigns, automation workflows, and audience segmentation. Best for bootstrapped startups.[3]
- HubSpot Marketing Hub (Free tools; $50/mo starter): Lead nurturing, ad management, and analytics integration. Targets early-stage SaaS (under 50 users).[3]
- Sendinblue (Brevo) (Free up to 300 emails/day; $25/mo starter): SMS/email automation and transactional emails. Affordable for small marketing teams.[3]
•Customer Support:
- Freshdesk (Free for 10 agents; $15/agent/mo growth): Omnichannel ticketing, AI automation, and self-service portal. Perfect for startups with high-volume support.[1][4]
- Zendesk (Free trial; $19/agent/mo suite): Advanced ticketing, chatbots, and analytics. Suited for SaaS startups (5-50 agents).[1][3]
- Zoho Desk (Free for 3 agents; $14/agent/mo standard): Budget automation, multi-language support, and CRM sync. Targets small B2B SaaS.[1]

Enterprise SaaS Solutions
•Business Intelligence:
- Domo (Contact sales; starts ~$100/user/mo): Real-time dashboards, data integration from 1,000+ sources, and AI analytics. For large enterprises (500+ employees).[4]
- Torii (Custom pricing): AI spend optimization, shadow IT discovery, and access management. Ideal for mid-large enterprises managing 100+ apps.[2]
- Josys (Custom enterprise plans): Autonomous discovery, AI license optimization, and compliance monitoring. Targets enterprises with complex SaaS stacks.[2]
•HR & Payroll:
- CoreView (Custom for Microsoft 365; ~$10-20/user/mo): License optimization, security, and admin automation for M365-heavy orgs. Suited for mid-large enterprises (200+ users).[2]

- Gusto (Simple $40/mo base + $6/employee): Full payroll, benefits, and compliance. For growing enterprises (50+ employees).[3]
